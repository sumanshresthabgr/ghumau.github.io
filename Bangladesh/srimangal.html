Srimangal

About Srimangal

Known as the “Tea Capital of Bangladesh”, Srimangal is a peaceful hill town in the Sylhet Division. It’s surrounded by endless tea gardens, rolling hills, and tranquil forests — an ideal destination for nature lovers.

Travel Highlights

  • Tea Gardens: Visit lush green plantations and learn how tea is processed.
  • Lawachara National Park: A tropical rainforest home to rare wildlife.
  • Madhabpur Lake: Beautiful lake surrounded by hills.
  • Indigenous Villages: Experience Manipuri and Khasia cultures.
  • Seven-Layer Tea: A unique tea experience only found here.

Best Time to Visit

October to February is ideal for exploring tea gardens and forests.

Things to Do

  • Walk through green tea estates
  • Spot wildlife in Lawachara Forest
  • Cycling around peaceful rural roads
  • Drink the seven-layer tea

How to Reach

Srimangal is 190 km from Dhaka, easily reachable by train or bus.

Why Visit?

Srimangal offers a refreshing escape into nature — full of greenery and calm village life.
