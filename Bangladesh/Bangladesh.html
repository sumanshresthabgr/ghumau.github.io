Welcome to Bangladesh

Bandarban

It is one of the most scenic hill districts of Bangladesh, lies in the Chittagong Hill Tracts region. It’s a paradise of rolling hills, lush forests, and vibrant tribal cultures.

Cox’s Bazar

It is located in southeastern Bangladesh along the Bay of Bengal, is world-renowned for having the longest natural sea beach

Srimangal

It is nown as the “Tea Capital of Bangladesh”, Srimangal is a peaceful hill town in the Sylhet Division.

Sundarbans

It is the world’s largest mangrove forest, shared between Bangladesh and India. This UNESCO World Heritage Site is famous for its Royal Bengal Tigers and diverse wildlife.
